Korean Festival Week is one of the biggest events in Monash University, held by Monash Street Dance Society and Monash Korean Club. The event including 4 days campus-wide Bazaar, Korean culture night and K-pop dance cover competition. This event earned a pure profit of more than 5k for our club.
